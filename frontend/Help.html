One token means that one analysis can be done.

Analysis type is a method of X-ray evaluation. It requires certain landmarks to be placed and measurements created and reports delivered.

Patient identifier needs to be written before the image is sent to tracing. Most usualy this is going to be the patient name, family name, or registration number or other identifier in patient records.

Cepheo will take the image and send the result of our analysis in the result page.

Cepheo accepts different types of raster formats such as for example: jpg, png and also 2D Dicom format.
